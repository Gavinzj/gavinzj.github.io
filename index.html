About me

I am a Ph.D. graduate from The Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. CHENG, Hong.

Previously, I received a Bachelor of Science degree from The Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. XU, Jianliang, and Prof. LU, Hua.

Research Interests

  • Generative AI
  • Mathematical Reasoning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Diffusion Large Language Models
  • Graph Data Mining

My research focuses on advancing artificial intelligence in real-world applications, particularly in the areas of mathematical reasoning and scientific discovery. I work on developing novel approaches for large language models, combining reinforcement learning and diffusion techniques to enhance AI capabilities in complex reasoning tasks.
