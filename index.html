About me

I am currently an AI researcher in Hong Kong. I received my Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management, supervised by Prof. CHENG, Hong. I completed my Bachelor of Computer Science at Hong Kong Baptist University in 2019, Department of Computer Science, under the supervision of Prof. XU, Jianliang and Prof. LU, Hua. During my academic journey, I was a visiting researcher at University of Auckland in 2024 and Aalborg University in 2018.
